# How AI at Swiggy is transforming convenience

_Until about two years back, Swiggy was a brand that’s synonymous with food delivery. We prided ourselves as being called ‘Hunger Saviours’ and this continues to resonate among many of our customers. Today, while the narrative around ‘delivering unparalleled convenience’ in the space of food continues, we have evolved much beyond the realm of hyperlocal food delivery. Here’s a sneak peek into it._

![image](../images/6f7175bac4d99bf6.png)

The nature of convenience is quite different in today’s pandemic era. Safety, health, and staying at home are the new normal, and this makes access to otherwise routine, on-ground services and products even more necessary, and difficult. Access to daily grocery, wellness products, and even medicines have found their way to the top of everyday checklists, but for Swiggy, this imminent need was already in the works, much before the pandemic.

In 2018, the vision of delivering ‘Unparalleled Convenience’ took to a rather refreshing route. We stepped into the shoes of our consumers and started understanding the aspects that could potentially make their lives easier while giving them time that was otherwise lost for routine use-cases. The outcome was the birth of Swiggy Stores, Swiggy Go (Now Genie), and Swiggy Daily, each of these product offerings designed to solve specific life scenarios for them, accessible to millions of consumers across the country. Sounds hunky-dory but wait! Our spidey senses also knew that with great business growth comes great complexity.

And to solve that complexity, we turned to our tech and AI teams. In this talk, our CTO Dale Vaz unravels the nuances of the complexity and our approaches into solving it. So sit back, order a cuppa on Swiggy and soak in the presentation.
