# Arcee AI Introduces Arcee-Nova: A New Open-Sourced Language Model based on Qwen2-72B and Approaches GPT-4 Performance Level

Arcee AI introduced [**Arcee-Nova**](https://blog.arcee.ai/introducing-arcee-nova/), a groundbreaking achievement in open-source artificial intelligence. Following their previous release, Arcee-Scribe, Arcee-Nova has quickly established itself as the highest-performing model within the open-source domain. Evaluated on the same stack as the OpenLLM Leaderboard 2.0, Arcee-Nova’s performance approaches that of GPT-4 from May 2023, marking a significant milestone for Arcee AI and the AI community at large.

Arcee-Nova is a sophisticated amalgamation of the Qwen2-72B-Instruct model, merged with a custom model tuned on a generalist dataset mixture. This combination, enhanced by reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), has resulted in a model that excels in various domains. The model has been meticulously evaluated and has emerged as the top-performing open-source model on the OpenLLM Leaderboard 2.0 stack. This achievement underscores its advanced capabilities and potential to rival some of today’s most well-known AI models.

The technical foundation of Arcee-Nova is built upon the robust Qwen2-72B-Instruct model, which has been augmented with a custom-tuned model. This tuning process involved a diverse generalist dataset mixture, ensuring the model’s versatility across different applications. The availability of GGUF versions on platforms like Hugging Face further enhances its accessibility and usability for developers and researchers.

Arcee-Nova boasts an impressive array of capabilities that cater to a wide range of applications. These include:

- **Reasoning:** The model excels in logical reasoning tasks, making it suitable for complex problem-solving scenarios.

- **Creative Writing:** Arcee-Nova can produce high-quality creative content with advanced language generation capabilities.

- **Coding:** The model can assist in code generation and quality improvement, streamlining software development processes.

- **General Language Understanding:** Its comprehensive language understanding makes it effective in various communication and comprehension tasks.

The diverse capabilities of Arcee-Nova translate into numerous business applications, making it a valuable asset across different sectors. Some of the prominent applications include:

- **Customer Service:** Arcee-Nova can power advanced chatbots and virtual assistants, enhancing customer service interactions.

- **Content Creation:** The model can generate high-quality marketing materials and documentation, aiding content creators.

- **Software Development:** It can assist in code generation and quality checks, improving software development efficiency.

- **Data Analysis:** Arcee-Nova can enhance data interpretation and reporting, providing deeper insights for businesses.

- **Research and Development:** It supports literature reviews and hypothesis generation, accelerating research processes.

- **Legal and Compliance:** The model can analyze contracts and perform regulatory checks, ensuring compliance.

- **Education and Training:** Arcee-Nova can be used in adaptive learning systems to personalize student education.

In conclusion, Arcee AI has expressed gratitude to the open-source AI community and the Qwen team for their foundational work on Qwen2-72B. This collaborative spirit has been instrumental in the development and success of Arcee-Nova. Looking ahead, Arcee AI invites researchers, developers, and businesses to explore Arcee-Nova’s capabilities. The company remains committed to advancing open-source AI & looks forward to seeing how the community builds upon this innovative technology.
